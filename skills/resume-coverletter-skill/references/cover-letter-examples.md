## Opening Hooks {#opening-hooks}

### Knowledge Management / Technical Writer
- "The job description is specific about what good looks like: SOPs and knowledge articles that are accurate, current, and easy to find. That is work I have been doing."
- "I've been building and maintaining structured documentation for technical audiences for [X] years — setup guides, SOPs, workflow docs, and reference content that has to hold up when the source material changes."
- "Writing guidance that works for someone doing a task alone, with no support, is a different skill than writing blog content. It requires accuracy and completeness from the start."

### SEO Specialist
- "At [Company], I led an SEO initiative that doubled organic traffic in one year through targeted keyword research and on-page optimization."
- "I've raised domain authority from 20 to 35 and moved 30 target keywords to page one — not through tricks, but through sustained technical and content work."
- "Fueling organic growth through search is specific, measurable work. At [Company], I improved SERP rankings for 50+ keywords to page one in six months."

### Content Manager / Content Strategist
- "At [Company], I ran an editorial team of [X] writers and built a content calendar that doubled monthly readership in 18 months — through SEO-aligned content, not volume alone."
- "I've grown blogs from 5K to 20K monthly visits by building content strategies that serve both search engines and actual readers."
- "Crafting content that drives traffic and earns it are different problems. At [Company], I solved both."

### AI Engineer / ML Engineer
- "At [Company], I built a TensorFlow-based predictive model that improved forecast accuracy from 75% to 90% and cut client costs by 20%."
- "I've deployed NLP pipelines that automated text analysis and saved 10 engineering hours per week — not prototypes, production systems."
- "My background is in the full ML lifecycle: data preprocessing, model training, and cloud deployment on AWS and GCP."

## Full Example: Knowledge Management / Content Writer {#example-knowledge-management}

*Role: Robinhood Knowledge Management Content Writer*

---

Hiring Team, Knowledge Management
Robinhood — Denver, CO

I'm applying for the Knowledge Management Content Writer role on the Platforms team.

The job description is specific about what good looks like: SOPs and knowledge articles that are accurate, current, and easy to find. Content that holds up across product launches and process changes. Writing that works for CX and operations teams who need the right answer fast, not a document that buries it. That is work I have been doing.

I run OutClaw, a weekly AI newsletter that ships every Tuesday on a fixed structure. Same sections, same format, same editorial standard every issue. The writing covers fast-moving technical topics and has to be accurate enough for practitioners who check their sources. I've held that format and quality bar across 50+ issues without drift.

I also sell AutoAI, a catalog of technical digital products, each shipped with complete setup documentation. Those guides cover environment setup, API dependencies, configuration steps, and what to do when something breaks. They have to work for someone doing the task alone, with no support. When buyers email with questions, I update the docs. The feedback loop is direct and the fixes are specific.

I use AI tools daily, including Claude and ChatGPT, for writing, reviewing, and improving documentation. OutClaw covers these tools in depth every week, so my understanding of them is hands-on, not theoretical.

One more thing worth mentioning: I've been a Robinhood customer since 2018. I know the product from the user side, which is useful context when writing guidance that has to reflect how people actually interact with it.

The Robinhood role is a good fit for what I've been building toward: structured, maintained content for operational teams, in an environment where accuracy and findability matter. I'm based in Denver and can be in the office three or more days a week.

Thank you for your time.
