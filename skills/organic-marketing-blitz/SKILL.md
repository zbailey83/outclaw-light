## Outclaw-Specific Positioning for Every Channel

When executing any channel, anchor to Outclaw's identity:

- **Who we help:** Marketers, founders, operators, and builders actively using AI tools
- **What we are:** The sharpest, most practical AI tools newsletter — not a news aggregator, not a beginner blog
- **What we're not:** A generic "AI news" account. We have opinions. We do hands-on testing. We call out hype.
- **The pitch (one line):** "Outclaw covers what actually works in AI tools — tested, opinionated, no fluff."

Every piece of outreach, every Reddit post, every DM must reflect this positioning. Never pitch "subscribe to our newsletter." Always pitch the specific value they'd get.
