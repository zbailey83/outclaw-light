About OutClaw AI:
Newsletter for AI Practitioners.

OutClaw AI exists because most AI content is written by people who have never shipped anything with AI. We fix that every Tuesday.

Founder & Editor

Hey, I'm Zach Bailey.

I've spent the last three years deep in the weeds of AI — building workflows, testing models, breaking things, and occasionally shipping something that actually works.

OutClaw started as a way to send the stuff I was learning to a handful of friends who kept asking “what should I actually pay attention to this week?” That question is still the editorial filter for every issue.

I care about useful over impressive. Practical over theoretical. Alpha over hype. If you've ever read an AI newsletter and thought “that was a lot of words for not much” — this one's for you.

Our principles
What we believe
Practitioners over pundits
Every piece of advice comes from someone who has actually used the thing. No armchair analysis, no reheated press releases.

The AI space moves fast and most of it doesn't matter. We filter aggressively so you don't have to doomscroll to stay current.
Free forever, for real
