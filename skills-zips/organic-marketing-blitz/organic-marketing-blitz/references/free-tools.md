# Free Tools + Product Hunt & Directories

---

## Free Tools Strategy

### Why Build a Free Tool
A free tool builds trust faster than any amount of content. People find it through Google, use it, and discover Outclaw. Zero signup required = zero friction = maximum top-of-funnel reach.

### What Free Tools Outclaw Could Build

**AI Prompt Tools:**
- AI tool matcher quiz ("answer 5 questions, get your ideal AI stack")
- Prompt library / prompt quality checker
- AI output readability scorer
- "Is this AI-generated?" detector (novelty + utility)

**Newsletter/Content Tools:**
- Newsletter subject line tester
- AI tools ROI calculator ("how much time would [AI tool] save your team per week?")
- Content brief generator for AI topics

**Comparison Tools:**
- AI tool pricing comparison calculator (input your use case, see actual cost across tools)
- AI tool feature comparison checker

### How to Launch a Free Tool

1. Build it and put it on Outclaw's website — no signup required to use it
2. Post it to:
   - Product Hunt (as a standalone tool, not just Outclaw)
   - Hacker News "Show HN:"
   - r/ChatGPT, r/ClaudeAI, r/artificial (as a value post: "I built X, here's how it works")
   - Relevant communities (see `references/communities.md`)
3. The tool's page links to Outclaw newsletter prominently but without being obnoxious
4. Add an email capture *after* someone uses the tool: "Want more tools and takes like this? Outclaw covers AI tools for practitioners weekly."

### The Failure Mode
Building a tool and hiding it. The tool is worthless if nobody finds it. Submit it everywhere on launch week. Treat the launch like a mini Product Hunt launch.

## Product Hunt + Directories

### The Truth About Product Hunt
Product Hunt gives you a spike, not a strategy. The real value is:
- The backlink (high-authority domain)
- The social proof badge ("Featured on Product Hunt")
- The press and newsletter coverage the launch unlocks

Traffic churns in 48 hours. The brand signal lasts for years.

### How to Launch Right

**Before:**
- Build a community of supporters who will upvote on launch day
- Use your existing subscribers + Reddit community + Discord/Slack communities
- Tell them 3–5 days before: "We're launching on Product Hunt on [day] — if you've found Outclaw useful, an upvote would mean a lot: [link]"
- DM your most engaged users directly to ask for support

**During:**
- Post on all social channels on launch day
- Reply to every single comment on Product Hunt (shows engagement, improves ranking)
- Post in communities: "We're on Product Hunt today — any feedback welcome"

**After:**
- Use the momentum to pitch AI/tech newsletters for coverage ("just launched on PH, here's the story")
- Add the Product Hunt badge to the Outclaw website
- Write a "what we learned from our PH launch" post (build in public + content recycling)

### Directories to Submit Outclaw To

Submit to these for backlinks + discoverability. Takes 30 minutes total:
- There's An AI For That (theresanaiforthat.com)
- Futurepedia
- AI Tools Directory
- Toolify.ai
- Insidr.ai
- Newsletter directories: Substack's discover, Beehiiv's directory, Newsletter Hunt, Letterdrop

Each submission = a backlink + potential discovery by their audience.
